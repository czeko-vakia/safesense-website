Frequently asked

How does SafeSense detect alcohol impairment?

We use facial microsignals and behavioral cues correlated with impairment. Models fuse these cues with temporal patterns. Flags are for probable impairment to allow safe intervention; it is not a breathalyzer.

Is driver data private?

Yes. On-device inference, configurable retention, role-based access and enterprise on-prem options are supported.
